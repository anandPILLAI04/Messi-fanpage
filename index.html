THE JOURNEY

Follow the path of a boy from Rosario who conquered the footballing cosmos.

1987 - 2000

Rosario Beginnings

Born in Rosario, Argentina. Leo begins playing for local club Newell's Old Boys. At age 10, he is diagnosed with a growth hormone deficiency, paving a difficult path forward.

Newell's Rosario
2000 - 2004

La Masia Scaffolding

FC Barcelona's sporting director Charly Rexach offers a contract signed on a paper napkin. The Messi family moves to Spain, and Leo joins the legendary La Masia academy, quickly outclassing older cohorts.

La Masia Barcelona
2004 - 2008

The Breakthrough

Debuts under Frank Rijkaard. Scores his first senior goal against Albacete from a Ronaldinho chip. Leads Argentina to Olympic Gold in Beijing (2008) and inherits the famous #10 shirt from Ronaldinho.

Debut Olympic Gold
2008 - 2012

Pep's False Nine & Glory

Under Pep Guardiola, Messi is moved to the "False Nine" position. Barcelona achieves an unprecedented Sextuple. Messi wins 4 consecutive Ballons d'Or and finishes 2012 with an astronomical 91 goals.

Sextuple 4x Ballon d'Or
2013 - 2017

The MSN Era

Teaming up with Luis Suárez and Neymar Jr, Messi orchestrates the devastating 'MSN' front three. They secure another Treble in 2015, capturing the Champions League and his 5th Ballon d'Or.

MSN Trio Treble 2015
2018 - 2021

Captaincy & Farewell

Appointed Barca captain, Messi carries the club through challenging years. He secures his 6th & 7th Ballon d'Or. In 2021, he wins his first senior international trophy (Copa América) before a tearful farewell to Barcelona due to financial constraints.

Copa América PSG Transfer
2022

World Cup Coronation

In Qatar 2022, Lionel Messi guides Argentina to its third FIFA World Cup. Scoring in every knockout stage and twice in the final, he wins the Golden Ball and cements his status as the undisputed GOAT.

World Champion Golden Ball
2023 - Present

Miami Magic & 8th Ballon d'Or

Messi moves to Inter Miami CF, immediately transforming the MLS club and winning the Leagues Cup. He captures a historic 8th Ballon d'Or and continues to delight fans worldwide.

Inter Miami 8th Ballon d'Or
